## Full Description (both stores)
Get everything you need to know about the Lansing Car Ferry — the free car ferry crossing the Mississippi River between Lansing, Iowa and De Soto, Wisconsin.

Features:
- Current ferry schedule with Wisconsin and Iowa departure times
- Live traffic camera feeds from Iowa DOT showing the ferry landings and queue
- Vehicle size and weight restrictions
- Holiday hours and commuter priority information
- Allowed and prohibited vehicle types
- Direct links to the ferry Facebook page for real-time service updates
- Ferry landing locations with one-tap navigation to Maps
- Links to 511 Iowa Traffic and Iowa DOT information
- Available in English, Spanish, French, Arabic, Somali, and Chinese

The Lansing Car Ferry is a free, publicly operated car ferry providing passage across the Mississippi River while the Blackhawk Bridge undergoes reconstruction. This app is an unofficial community resource — always check the ferry's Facebook page for the latest service updates and schedule changes.

No account required. No ads. No data collection. Just ferry info.
