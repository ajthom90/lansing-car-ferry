Privacy Policy

Lansing Ferry
Last updated: February 25, 2026

Overview

Lansing Ferry ("the App") provides information about the Lansing, Iowa car ferry service, including schedules, live camera feeds, and frequently asked questions. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App does not require account creation, login, or any form of user registration. Specifically:

  • No personal data (name, email, phone number) is collected.
  • No location data is collected or tracked.
  • No usage analytics or tracking identifiers are collected.
  • No cookies or similar technologies are used.
  • No data is shared with third parties for advertising or analytics purposes.

Network Requests

The App makes the following network requests solely to display content:

  • Ferry information: The App downloads publicly available ferry schedule and information data from a static file hosted on a content delivery network (jsDelivr/GitHub).
  • Live camera feeds: The App streams publicly available video from Iowa Department of Transportation (Iowa DOT) traffic cameras. These streams are accessed directly from Iowa DOT servers.

No personal information is transmitted as part of these requests beyond standard HTTP headers sent by the device's networking stack.

Third-Party Links

The App contains links to external websites and services, including:

  • The Lansing Car Ferry Facebook page
  • 511 Iowa Traffic
  • Iowa Department of Transportation
  • Apple Maps or Google Maps (for ferry landing locations)

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services.

Data Storage

The App caches ferry information locally on your device to improve performance and allow limited offline access. This cached data contains only publicly available ferry schedule and operational information. No personal data is stored. You can clear this cache by deleting and reinstalling the App.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
