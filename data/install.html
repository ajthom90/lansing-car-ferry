Lansing Ferry for Android

Get live camera feeds, schedules, and ferry information for the Lansing, Iowa car ferry — right on your Android device.

Download APK

How to Install

  1. Tap Download APK above.
  2. When the download finishes, tap the notification or open the file from your Downloads folder.
  3. If prompted, allow your browser to install apps from unknown sources:
    • You'll see a message like "For your security, your phone is not allowed to install unknown apps from this source."
    • Tap Settings, then enable Allow from this source.
    • Go back and tap Install.
  4. Once installed, open Lansing Ferry from your app drawer.

Requirements

  • Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later
